# SIVA-GROSS-
 Global Sales &amp; Profit "Command Center" The Simple Goal: I took a mountain of messy raw data and turned it into this clean, interactive dashboard. It’s designed to show a business owner exactly where their money is coming from and where they can grow.   "Turning raw numbers into clear, visual stories that help you make faster, smarter decisions."
Project Name: Global Sales & Profit Intelligence Dashboard
The Simple Breakdown:
I transformed a massive, complex dataset into this all-in-one interactive command center. It allows business owners to see exactly how much they are selling, where their profit is coming from, and how discounts are affecting their bottom line—all in real-time.

What makes this "Pro":

Instant Insights: A clean "Executive Summary" showing $128M in Profit and $127M in Sales at a single glance.

Global Tracking: High-level analysis across the USA, Canada, France, Germany, and Mexico.

Smart Filtering: Includes custom sidebars to filter by month and slicers for Segments (Small Business vs. Enterprise) and Discount Levels.

Visual Storytelling: Uses heatmaps and trend lines to show that October and December are the peak performing months.

The Result: No more digging through messy spreadsheets. One look at this dashboard tells you exactly where the business is winning and where it needs to improve.
Business Problems Solved
Every "Pro" project must solve a problem. This dashboard addresses:

Manual Reporting Fatigue: Replaced hours of manual Excel work with an automated, refreshable system.

Data Silos: Combined sales, logistics (transaction data), and manufacturing costs into one view.

Blind Decision Making: Instead of "guessing" which months are busiest, the data proves that October is a critical peak, allowing for better inventory planning.

Pricing Inefficiency: Clearly shows which product segments are underperforming despite high sales volume, enabling price adjustments.
. Key Highlights & Business Value
Discount Sensitivity Analysis: The dashboard reveals how different discount tiers (High, Medium, Low) correlate with profit margins, preventing "revenue leakage" from over-discounting.

Geographical Intelligence: Identified the United States as the primary revenue driver, while spotting growth opportunities in the Mexico and Germany markets.

Profitability Tracking: The "Total Sale vs. Sum of Profit" gauge provides an instant health check on business efficiency.
